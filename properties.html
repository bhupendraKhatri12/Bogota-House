About us

Kathmandu HOUSES was created with the purpose of offering a platform where homeowners and sellers in Kathamndu can post their properties to sale or rent. We avoid charging monthly fees and try to keep our business model as simple as possible. Everyone can post their property for free here and only pay if they want it marked as urgent or premium. The urgent and premium flags last forever and there is no need to renew them.

Thousands of sold properties

More the 10 thousans sold properties. It could be an apartment, a flat, a house or land for sale, you name it, Kathmandu HOUSES can have it listed and available for viewing. Agents are welcome to list their properties. We aim to continue delivering exceptional quality services and help sellers find buyers and landlords find tenants.
